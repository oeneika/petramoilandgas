Services

We apply a very encompassing approach to every oil and/or gas project we are involved in Among other services, Petram Oil & Gas provides static and dynamic reservoir characterization, enhanced oil recovery (EOR), drilling optimization, exploration and production (E&P), procurement services, quality control and corporate and project finance advisory.

Static and Dynamic Reservoir Characterization

We have integrated a comprehensive geo-science data integration approach that has applications in all phases of the exploration and production lifecycle. From survey design and seismic data processing to seismic Interpretation, reservoir characterization and dynamic reservoir Modeling, we offer a project portfolio with optimized drilling programs and enhanced oil Recovery (EOR) solutions (EOR). The following are technical key elements of our geo-science workflow:

a) Survey Design;

b) Seismic data processing;

c) Well log analysis;

d) Anisotropy estimation;

e) Pre-stack depth migration;

f) Seismic Interpretation;

g) Fracture Intensity maps;

h) Petrophysics;

i) Reservoir Characterization;

j) Oil Reserve Estimation;

k) Dynamic Reservoir Modeling;

l) Geomechanics.

Enhanced Oil Recovery

We use secondary and tertiary oil recovery (enhanced oil recovery [EOR]) techniques in our projects, to change the properties of the hydrocarbons. We have multi-functional chemical products to ensure the flow and optimization of oil production, developed for the control of, among other things, crude blockage with asphaltenes, paraffins and scale deposits, applicable to:

a) Multilayer deposits and deposits with intervals long production runs;
b) Horizontal and vertical wells;
c) Surface equipment: separators, vessels, pipelines and gas lines;
d) Non-reactive matrix stimulation;
e) Cleaning tubulars, etc.

Drilling Optimization

Our drilling optimization services are based on our experience in field operations and the use of cutting-edge technology. Through the use of logically designed engineering processes, and supported by years of drilling experience, we analyze data from multiple offset wells to understand the potential performance barriers that can result in non-productive time or invisible lost time. All this in order to design a drilling plan that leverages on advanced technologies and unmatched experience, resulting in a superior performance.

Exploration and Production

At Petram, we use our experience from the business and project concept, the exploration and development phase, up until completion and production phases, providing informed, independent road maps to prop up projects efficiently, supporting the entire exploration and production value chain. We assist and/or partner with clients from the business and project concept, helping to quantify the benefits, create a robust delivery plan, and execute the plan to time and budget, Our E& approach seeks to improve any project’s performance, evaluate any strategic initiative and assist in the due diligence process by helping to define and implement better work practices, technical processes and technology solutions.

Procurement Services

We offer reliable procurement and high quality assurance services for the oil and gas industry at competitive prices. We guarantee customer satisfaction in sourcing and purchasing services (i.e. purchase planning, specifications determination, supplier research and selection, financing and price negotiation), through quality assurance and quality control, monitoring constantly each step of the process.

Corporate and project finance

Corporate and project finance advisory. Development of feasibility studies, business cases, creditworthy and bankable financial proposals and/or structures to prop up oil and gas projects and businesses, among others.
