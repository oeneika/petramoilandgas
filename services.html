Our Services

Services

We apply a very encompassing approach to every oil and/or gas project we are involved in Among other services, Petram Oil & Gas provides static and dynamic reservoir characterization, enhanced oil recovery (EOR), drilling optimization, exploration and production (E&P), procurement services, quality control and corporate and project finance advisory.

Static and Dynamic Reservoir Characterization

We have integrated a comprehensive geo-science data integration approach that has applications in all phases of the exploration and production lifecycle. From survey design and seismic data processing to seismic Interpretation, reservoir characterization and dynamic reservoir Modeling, we offer a project portfolio with optimized drilling programs and enhanced oil Recovery (EOR) solutions (EOR). Drilling Optimization

Our drilling optimization services are based on our experience in field operations and the use of cutting-edge technology.

Through the use of logically designed engineering processes, and supported by years of drilling experience, we analyze data from multiple offset wells to understand the potential performance barriers that can result in non-productive time or invisible lost time. All this in order to design a drilling plan that leverages on advanced technologies and unmatched experience, resulting in a superior performance.
