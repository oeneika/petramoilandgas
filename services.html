Exotermic Reaction System

It consists in the injection of chemical compositions at flow rates and pressures lower than the fracture pressure of the reservoir; these treatments are previously selected through an evaluation process that involves engineering studies and laboratory tests, effectively designing the treatment and making it profitable. This technology basically generates heat with the reaction of nitrogen gas resulting in a thermochemical synergistic system for the elimination of damage from organic deposits, improving the rate of production. The thermochemical system generates temperatures between 90 and 120 ° C.

Enhanced Oil Recovery

We use secondary and tertiary oil recovery (enhanced oil recovery [EOR]) techniques in our projects, to change the properties of the hydrocarbons. We have multi-functional chemical products to ensure the flow and optimization of oil production, developed for the control of, among other things, crude blockage with asphaltenes, paraffins and scale deposits, applicable to:

a) Multilayer deposits and deposits with intervals long production runs;
b) Horizontal and vertical wells;
c) Surface equipment: separators, vessels, pipelines and gas lines;
d) Non-reactive matrix stimulation;
e) Cleaning tubulars, etc.

Drilling Optimization

Our drilling optimization services are based on our experience in field operations and the use of cutting-edge technology. Through the use of logically designed engineering processes, and supported by years of drilling experience, we analyze data from multiple offset wells to understand the potential performance barriers that can result in non-productive time or invisible lost time. All this in order to design a drilling plan that leverages on advanced technologies and unmatched experience, resulting in a superior performance.
