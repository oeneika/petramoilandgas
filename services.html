Our Services

We apply a very encompassing approach to every oil and/or gas project we are involved in. Among other services, Petram Oil & Gas provides static and dynamic reservoir characterization, enhanced oil recovery (EOR), drilling optimization, exploration and production (E&P), procurement services, quality control and corporate and project finance advisory.

Drilling Optimization

Our drilling optimization services are based on our experience in field operations and the use of cutting-edge technology. Through the use of logically designed engineering processes, and supported by years of drilling experience, we analyze data from multiple offset wells to understand the potential performance barriers that can result in non-productive time or invisible lost time. All this in order to design a drilling plan that leverages on advanced technologies and unmatched experience, resulting in a superior performance.
